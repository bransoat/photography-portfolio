Justin Time

I am 51 years old, born and raised in Los Angeles, and currently living in Virginia. I studied fine art and photography in college, eventually graduating from UCLA with a degree in Art. Though my emphasis was in Drawing and Painting, I took many photography classes which turned my art towards a more gritty, realistic direction. After college, I put painting and photography to the side. Around 2007, I took up photography again, starting with a Canon Powershot A1000. After a year of only using that, I got my first DSLR, a Pentax Kx. From there I got a Pentax K-01, then upgraded to a Nikon D610.At the beginning of 2018, after shooting digital for over 10 years, I picked up a Nikon F100 someone has gifted to me, and began exclusively shooting film.My current set up included the Nikon F100, and Canonet QL17 GIII, and a Mamiya C220 medium format film camera. I process my 35mm and 120 film at home. I scan my 35mm with a Plustek 8100 dedicated film scanner, and I do DSLR scanning for my 120 film, processing it in Negative Lab Pro. In terms of style, I find myself more comfortable being a documentarian rather than an "artist". I like trying to shoot places and people as they are, rather than though some stylized vision of what they could be.
